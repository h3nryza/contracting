# What Next (Plain English)

1. **Fill in the Blanks.** Add the real company names, rates, currencies, contact people, bundle sizes, and payment terms everywhere you see a placeholder.
2. **Get a Legal Check.** Send the filled-in drafts to a South African lawyer to confirm the wording works under local law and your industry rules.
3. **Align Your Team.** Share the bundle limits, RACI chart, and monitoring promises with your service team so everyone knows the boundaries.
4. **Review with the Client.** Walk the client through the documents, confirm their coordinator and escalation contacts, and tweak anything they reasonably need.
5. **Sign and Store.** Use e-signatures, keep the signed PDFs in your contract system, and add reminders for renewals and compliance reviews.
