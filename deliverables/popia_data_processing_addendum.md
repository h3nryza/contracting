# POPIA Processing Addendum

This POPIA Processing Addendum ("POPIA Addendum") forms part of any agreement between AffinityLogic ("Operator") and __________________ ("Responsible Party") where the Operator processes personal information as defined in the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 ("POPIA").

## 1. Purpose and Scope
Operator processes personal information solely to deliver managed environment services, proactive monitoring, incident triage, and related professional services within the contracted five-hour monthly bundles, plus any add-on bundles purchased by the Responsible Party.

## 2. Operator Undertakings
Operator shall:
1. Process personal information only with the knowledge or authorization of the Responsible Party and for the limited purposes described in the principal agreement.
2. Treat personal information as confidential and bind all personnel with appropriate confidentiality obligations.
3. Implement and maintain appropriate, reasonable technical and organizational measures to prevent loss, damage, unauthorized destruction, and unlawful access, as required under sections 19–22 of POPIA.
4. Promptly notify the Responsible Party of any security compromise in writing, providing sufficient detail for the Responsible Party to meet sections 22 notification duties.
5. Assist the Responsible Party, at its cost, with data subject requests or regulatory inquiries relating to the processed personal information.
6. Not transfer personal information outside South Africa unless lawful transfer mechanisms exist and have been communicated to the Responsible Party.

## 3. Sub-Operators
Operator shall not appoint a sub-operator without prior written authorization from the Responsible Party. When authorized, Operator shall ensure sub-operators are bound by written obligations no less protective than those set out herein and remains responsible for their performance.

## 4. Retention and Destruction
Upon termination or expiry of the Agreement—or earlier at the Responsible Party's written instruction—Operator shall return or destroy personal information, save where retention is required by law. Any retained information remains subject to this POPIA Addendum.

## 5. Audit Rights
Upon reasonable notice, the Responsible Party may audit Operator's compliance during normal business hours without unreasonably disrupting operations. Operator may satisfy audit obligations by providing recent third-party certifications or summaries of security controls.

## 6. Liability and Indemnity
Each Party bears liability arising from its own negligent or willful acts or omissions. Operator indemnifies Responsible Party for claims directly resulting from Operator's breach of POPIA Addendum obligations, subject to the limitations contained in the main Agreement.

## 7. Governing Law
This POPIA Addendum is governed exclusively by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, and the Parties submit to the jurisdiction of the High Court of South Africa (Gauteng Division, Johannesburg).

## 8. Conflict
Where the POPIA Addendum conflicts with other terms relating to personal information, this POPIA Addendum prevails.
